Academics

The campus of BANDHGORA ANCHAL (X-CLASS) VIDYALAYA (H.S) is situated at Gai-Chandra Village under the newly created District of Jhargram. The area is a place of natural beauty. The Vidyalaya is set up in a campus of 4.22 acres of land donated by the under-privileged Chalak(s). In 1965, the retired Principal named Sri Panchkari Dey felt the need of a Vidyalaya for the Villagers of Bandhgora Anchal No.2. The renowned educationist Sri Dey's endeavour resulted in the establishment of the Vidyalaya at Gai-Chandra with a view to spread education in a backward area. The School building was started in 1965. Founded on 01/01/1965, BAVHS grew into education centre , offering to the Madhyamik passed students a whole array of subjects from two disciplines of Arts & Science. The Vidyalaya is affiliated to WBBSE (1965) & WBCHSE(2005). Computer and internet facility is available for the staff and the students within the campus. Our Campus is equipped with water purifiers to provide safe drinking water to students and staff. Our Vidyalaya has the VISHAKHA committee. The whole campus is under CC.camera surveillance.

Rules & Regulations

The students admitted to this Vidyalaya are to abide by the following rules and regulations issued by the Headmaster from time to time. No student can plead ignorance of such rules and regulations and Notices.

  1. A minimum of 75% attendance in classes (Theory and Project /Practical separately) in all subjects is mandatory. Vidyalaya authority may excuse shortage in attendance upto 15% under special circumstances on payment of condonation fee.
  2. If a student is absent from classes without any application and without proper reason for more than 1 month , his/her name may be struck off the Vidyalaya rolls.
  3. A student must take all class Unit Tests.
  4. During the Parent-Teacher meetings (at least twice in a year) only the Guardians /parents whose signature appear in the admission Register, will be permitted to attend the said meetings.
  5. If a student has any complaint / grievance / suggestion, he/she may write it down and drop it in the Complaint Box installed in the Verandah of the Vidyalaya Office.
  6. If a student has any complaint / grievance / suggestion, he/she may write it down and drop it in the Complaint Box installed in the Verandah of the Vidyalaya Office.
  7. The Vidyalaya authority hold the right to issue TC/Rusticate a student if they consider such action is necessary in the interest of the Vidyalaya.
  8. The Vidyalaya helps the SC/ST/OBC students obtain the Govt. stipends/ scholarships.
  9. Applications for free/ half free studentship from poor / donor families are considered by the School.
  10. Every year Kanyashree prakalpa is going on without any break. Girl students are enjoying the K1 & K2 .
  11. Always carry the Identity Cards issued from the Vidyalaya.
  12. Students are advised not to use the Mobile Phones in a classroom.
  13. You are required to avoid the misconduct / misbehaviour of any kind towards teacher /an employee / any fellow student & causing any damage to furniture / any other property of the Vidyalaya.

Eligibility

A student can get himself/herself admitted in Class XI if he/she obtains pass marks in the last qualifying examination. A candidate shall be allowed to take up Geography or Science Subjects if he/she obtains 65% in Geography or 75% in Science subjects in M.P. respectively.
